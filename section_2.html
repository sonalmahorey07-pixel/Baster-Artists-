82.8% of artisans report that other family members also participate in craft-making. Combined with the finding that 93% learned through family tradition, this data strongly confirms the inter-generational transmission model of these art forms.

This is a key indicator for classifying the craft as Cultural Heritage (Family-Transmitted) rather than institutionally preserved.
